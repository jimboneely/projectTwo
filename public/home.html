About PS 202

A quality education should begin early in your child's life. PS 202 is here to equip parents with vital information about Child development centers in the District of Columbia. Our data on these centers was provided by the Office of the State Superintendent of Education and the dc.gov "Open Data" initiative. Search for centers and even schedule a site visit or meeting using our customizable email templates.
